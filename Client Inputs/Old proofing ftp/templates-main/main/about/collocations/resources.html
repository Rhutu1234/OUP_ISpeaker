Oxford Collocations Dictionary: Resources

Print out exercises and browse reference pages from the dictionary.


Study pages

Using the Study pages, you can complete exercises on how to understand and use different types of collocations entries (for example ‘verb entries’), as well as exercises on collocations for vocabulary topics (for example ‘education’).

You can mark your own work using the answer key.

Special pages

Do you need to brainstorm vocabulary for an essay or are you studying a particular topic? The Special pages will be useful.

Each Special page pulls together collocations on a particular topic, such as business, meetings and sport.
