What is the iSpeaker?

The Oxford iSpeaker is designed for learners of English to help you speak more accurately and fluently in a variety of situations. The three sections each focus on a different aspect of spoken English - from individual sounds and words, to phrases, to longer speaking tasks. Watch videos, listen to audio, analyze the language used, and practise sounds and phrases yourself through guided activities.

You can choose British or American English on this page, and all video and audio content will follow this choice until you return to this page to change it.
Where is my saved work? Any content that you saved in the previous version of the iSpeaker (i.e. before April 2021) can be temporarily accessed here. The previous version of the iSpeaker will be available until 31 December 2021.

iSpeaker 是什么？

Oxford iSpeaker 专为英语学习者设计，旨在帮助您在各种情景中更加准确、流利地使用英语。软件分为三个版块，每个版块针对英语口语的一个方面——从单个语音、单词，到短语，再到更长的口语表达。您可以按引导自主看视频，听音频，分析所用的语言，并练习语音和短语。 您可以在本页面选择英式或美式英语，所有的视频和音频内容将以所选语言播放，也可返回本页面重新选择。
